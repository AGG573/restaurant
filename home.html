Something Fishy is the Northeast's premier gourmet seafood restaurant located in iconic Beach City, Maine. Serving everything from Dragon Rolls to Lobster Fra Diavolo, always with locally caught fresh fish and the highest quality ingredients. Since opening their resturant 2 years ago, co-owners, founders and sisters Chrissy and Ilana Clarke have been featured on Forbes 30 Under 30 and Something Fishy was ranked 4th on The World's 50 Best Restaurants list. Whether you're a hungry local or a curious tourist, we promise that dining at Something Fishy is a culinary experience like no other. Make a reservation today!
